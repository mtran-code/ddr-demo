Drug Dose Response Curve Fitting

Interactive tool for fitting monophasic and biphasic dose-response curves

Monophasic

How to use:

• Click anywhere on the plot to add data points (The tool is intended to be a demo with fuzzy fake data)

• After 5+ points, a curve will be automatically fitted

• Toggle between monophasic and biphasic fits

• Switch algorithms to compare fitting methods
